```
# Spanish Basics – Lecture 1

## Articles + Nouns
el hombre → the man  
el niño → the boy  
  sounds like: el ninio  
la niña → the girl  
  sounds like: la ninia  
la mujer → the woman  
una mujer → a woman  
un hombre → a man  
la manzana → the apple  
el pan → the bread  
la leche → the milk  
el agua → the water  
  sounds like: el aua  

## Pronouns + Verb "ser" (to be)
yo soy → I am  
tú eres → you are  
  sounds like: too‿eh eres  
ella es → she is  
yo soy, tú eres, ella es → I am, you are, she is  

## Verb "comer" (to eat)
yo como → I eat  
tú comes → you eat  
él come → he eats  
Ella come → she eats  

## Verb "beber" (to drink)
yo bebo agua → I drink water  
```
